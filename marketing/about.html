REALTOR® + Builder

About Brandon Wilcox Home Group

One accountable partner in Grand Rapids and across West Michigan.

The Brandon Wilcox Home Group is led by Brandon Wilcox, a licensed REALTOR® and a licensed builder. We operate on a simple idea: clear scope and documented options. You get straight assessments, real numbers, and a plan aligned to your goal—buying smart, selling with a defined strategy, or improving after close.

Brandon blends field-tested construction experience with disciplined representation. Known for direct answers, practical scopes, and an ROI mindset, he focuses on protecting budget, reducing risk, and delivering results without drama across Grand Rapids and greater West Michigan.

Renovation is optional. We never push projects. We recommend right-sized work only when it measurably improves your net or reduces risk. If the smartest move is to list as-is or buy move-in ready, we’ll say so.

What we do

✦

Licensed Realtor® — Buyer & Seller Representation

The Brandon Wilcox Home Group delivers real estate strategy with real-world insight. As a licensed REALTOR® with hands-on construction experience, we help buyers see beyond the surface and guide sellers in preparing homes that stand out. Our pricing, staging, and negotiation approach is built to protect your investment and stay aligned to your goals. Whether you’re buying your first home or selling one you’ve outgrown, we offer straight answers, smart strategy, and steady communication—so you can move forward with clarity.

⬚

Handyman & Home Services — Make-Ready & Maintenance

Every home needs upkeep—and every deal depends on the details. Our handyman and home services team tackles punch lists, pre-listing repairs, and post-sale touch-ups to keep homes performing and transactions on track. Whether you're fixing a few small issues before going to market or settling into your new home, we offer quick, reliable solutions. With the Brandon Wilcox Home Group, small jobs don’t fall through the cracks—they build long-term value.

⟡

Construction & Renovation — Right-Sized Improvements

We don’t just build—we improve with purpose. The Brandon Wilcox Home Group specializes in value-driven updates that make homes more livable, sellable, and functional. From kitchen refreshes to whole-home updates, we tailor each project to your goals and budget. Our process is transparent and efficient, with clearly scoped plans, vetted local trades, and reliable timelines. The result? Improvements that enhance your home’s appeal without overbuilding or overspending.
